Board Game Geek: What makes a board game popular?

Board Games are one of my favourite hobbies - I enjoy many kinds of genres: Puzzle, Strategy, Card Games, and even a Party Game every once in a while. The ones you see on store shelves have evolved, from The Landlord's Game (1904) - the origin of the world-famous Monopoly, into a massive variety of genres. The market for board games has grown exponentially since then, with a massive increase of published games in the years after 2010. The goal of this project is to have a look at the modern board gaming scene, and how a budding designer could take insights from data and apply it to their design process.

I answer questions like:

  • What do people like in board games?
  • What is the most popular game?
  • If I wanted to design a popular board game, where should I start my research?
